Gen 3 Over-used

Competitive Analysis

This is my favorite metagame of competitive Pokemon, I find the elements and counterpicks of this metagame to be extremely fun.

The top threats in this metagame include:

  • Tyranitar
  • Zapdos
  • Metagross
  • Skarmory
  • Blissey

Gen 3 OU stands out in particular as a Spikes metagame, in which the Spikes entry hazard is one of the dominant forces upon gameplay. Spikes disincentivizes switch-stalling and greatly alters the metagame. Additionally as a permanent weather metagame, sandstorm stays up indefinitely, making Tyranitar potentially the most dominant pokemon of this metagame.

An important part of competitive Pokemon is teambuilding! You can have a team full of good pokemon, and still have a bad team.
